# Hello! I'm Jamie

I am a fullstack software developer based in California with over 10 years of experience building
performant experiences for the web. Currently I'm employed as a Senior Software Engineer at <a
href="https://www.thisdot.co" target="_blank" rel="noopener">This Dot</a>.

and Python; but also the frontend as well with React, Next.js and Angular. I create performant and

services from marketing campaign landing pages to online ordering systems.

If you want to see some of what I've done, then feel free to check out my [work

guild every week killing bosses and getting loot.
